If you decide to move forward with treatment that day, the actual procedure is quick. You'll leave with aftercare instructions and my direct contact info in case you have any questions.

Most clients describe it as a small pinch that lasts just a second. We use very fine needles and can apply numbing cream beforehand if you'd like. Most people are surprised by how quick and comfortable it is.

It depends on the type of filler and the area treated. Lip filler typically lasts 6-12 months, while cheek and jawline filler can last 12-18 months. Individual results vary based on metabolism and lifestyle. We'll discuss realistic timelines during your consultation.

Botox relaxes the muscles that cause wrinkles (like forehead lines and crow's feet). Fillers add volume to areas that have lost fullness or need enhancement (like lips, cheeks, and jawline). They work differently but complement each other beautifully. Many clients benefit from both.

Botox is priced at $12 per unit. The number of units needed varies per person and treatment area. A forehead treatment typically uses 10-30 units. We'll give you an exact quote during your free consultation so there are no surprises.

Most injectable treatments have minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness, swelling, or bruising at the injection sites, but this typically resolves within a few hours to a couple of days. Most clients return to normal activities immediately. We provide detailed aftercare instructions.
