My Story

Where Medical Expertise Meets Artistry

Welcome to Little Red Esthetics. As a licensed registered nurse and certified aesthetic injector, I bring together clinical precision and an artistic eye to help you look and feel your absolute best.

My journey into aesthetics started with a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel confident in their own skin. I saw how the right treatment, done well, could change the way someone carries themselves. That's what drives me every single day.

I specialize in injectable treatments that enhance your natural beauty rather than change who you are. My approach is always "less is more" -- I'd rather you leave looking like a refreshed, glowing version of yourself than someone different entirely.

Every face is unique, and every treatment plan I create reflects that. During your consultation, I'll take the time to understand your goals, assess your facial anatomy, and recommend a personalized plan that's right for you. No cookie-cutter treatments here.

My Promise to You

You'll always receive honest advice, a comfortable experience, and results that make you smile when you look in the mirror. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients, not just doing quick procedures.
