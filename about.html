About Little Red Esthetics

The story, the passion, and the purpose behind every treatment.

My Story

Hi, I'm Celeste

I'm an independent nurse injector and the face behind Little Red Esthetics. It all started from lash extensions and a deep love for helping people feel beautiful. That passion led me through nursing school with one goal in mind: to master the art of aesthetic injections.

With 8 years as a Master Medical Esthetician and 3 years as an Aesthetic RN with international training, I bring a unique combination of skincare expertise and medical precision to every treatment. I completed my nursing degree specifically to perfect the Russian lip technique, and I've been obsessed with delivering natural-looking results ever since.

I specialize in injectable treatments that enhance your natural beauty rather than change who you are. My approach is always "less is more." I'd rather you leave looking like a refreshed, glowing version of yourself than someone different entirely.

Every face is unique, and every treatment plan I create reflects that. During your consultation, I'll take the time to listen and make a plan that fits your own desired outcome. No cookie-cutter treatments here.

My Promise to You

You'll always receive honest advice, a comfortable experience, and results that make you smile when you look in the mirror. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients, not just doing quick procedures.
